Connecting the Web should be easy, free and collaborative.

Rhizi is an open source platform for enriching the Web with connections.

  • Refresh if blank

How it works

1. Choose what to connect
Pick two online resources or ideas.
2. Describe why
Assert how these two are connected.
3. Done!
Your connection becomes a part of an open and collective knowledge network.

Features

  • Real Time Collaboration
  • Graph, List and People Views
  • Powerful text and query language search

Mission

To connect the planet by empowering people with a new way of sharing, creating and exploring knowledge and insights. Create an open ecosystem around the technology. Open source and open data. Enabling the emergence of a global collective intelligence.
